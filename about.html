G'day, I'm Lisa Wolf

Photographer from the Southern Antipodes

I began photography with my parents' Box Brownie, with which I produced no particularly good photos. From there it was a series of 35mm Instamatics, the results of which have been lost to time. It wasn't until my first digital camera (A whole 3 Megapixels) from my then Housemate that I began to really get into photography, because now I could take a memory's card worth of photos in one hit. Then do it again, and again.
